```verilog
module comparator_8bit (input [7:0] A, B, output reg gt, eq, lt);
  
  assign gt = (A > B);
  assign eq = (A == B);
  assign lt = (A < B);
  
endmodule
```
This module takes two 8-bit inputs A and B. It then compares the values of A and B, setting the outputs 'gt', 'eq', and 'lt' to indicate whether A is greater than, equal to, or less than B respectively. The outputs are declared as reg because they will be driven by assignments inside an always block.
